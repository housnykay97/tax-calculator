In Kenya, workers in formal employment are taxed through Pay As You Earn (PAYE).

The formula for determining PAYE is based on Kenyan Tax Laws (Individual tax calculation).

For BASIC PAY CALCULATOR, use "Salary Type" options in form dropdowns.

As an employee, your employer will deduct this tax and remit it to KRA on your behalf through KRA Online Portal.

Other Deductions

  • NSSF: 6% of gross salary, min base KES 18,000. Follows NSSF Act schedule.
  • SHA: 2.75% of gross salary, minimum KES 300. Replaces NHIF from Oct 2024.
  • Housing Levy: 1.5% of gross salary when selected.

Note: SHA (Health) and Housing Levy are deductible before tax from Dec 2024. Insurance relief of 15% applies to SHIF. Estimates follow Finance Act 2024/2025. For official filing use KRA iTax.

How it works

  1. Enter monthly basic salary and benefits
  2. Select salary type, NSSF rate, and toggle SHA/Housing Levy.
  3. Choose round-off precision and click "Calculate Breakdown".
  4. View detailed breakdown and download as PDF.
